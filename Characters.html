Tricks n Treats
Tricks 'n Treats is a webcomic created by two aspiring artists and storytellers. It focuses on the adventures of two candy company owners named Don and Cecil. When a mysterious stranger appears at Cecil's vacation home one night, he finds himself transformed into his candy company's mascot, Muns - which he completely despises. When his rival Don comes to investigate, he finds himself falling into the bizarre web of happenstances when he himself is also transformed into a candy monster. With the strange outbreak spreading, it is up to Don and Cecil to stop the nefarious Randall Valentine from turning everyone in the world into cute and cuddly candy monsters!
